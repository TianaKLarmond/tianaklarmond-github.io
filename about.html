Meet the website creator, Tiana Larmond!



The short

Tiana Larmond is an aspiring front-end developer with a deep passion for the French language and occasionally entertains the idea of venturing into the field of database administration whenever she feels particularly ambitious.


The long

Tiana Larmond, a first-generation university student who was raised in a single-parent household, stands out not only for her strong determination but also for her impressive academic prowess. Currently in her final year pursuing a BSc in Computing at the University of Technology, Jamaica, she has consistently maintained a high GPA throughout her studies.

Alongside her dedication to academics, Tiana has showcased exceptional leadership skills. She has held numerous significant positions during her university tenure, including roles such as first-year representative and academic supervisor, among others. Her commitment to excellence and her ability to inspire others have made her a respected figure among her peers.

In her spare time, Tiana enjoys going on walks and practicing the French language, which she has developed a deep affinity for. These activities not only provide her with a refreshing break from her academic pursuits but also contribute to her personal growth and cultural enrichment.

Looking towards the future, Tiana's aspirations remain steadfast. She hopes to find happiness, establish a fulfilling career as a web developer, and contribute her skills to the growth of the tech industry. Tiana's passion for continuous learning, combined with her strong work ethic, positions her on a promising path to success.
